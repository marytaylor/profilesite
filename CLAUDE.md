## Site Sections (Agreed Plan)

### 1. Hero / Above the Fold
- Name, one-liner positioning statement, clear CTA
- Example tone: "AI PM | Builder | maryetaylor.com"
- Buttons: "View my work" and "Get in touch"

### 2. About
- Short narrative arc: technical background (physics) to data science to PM to building AI products
- Should feel like a person wrote it, not a LinkedIn profile
- Warm, confident, not corporate

### 3. Portfolio / Projects
- Historical work at companies (anonymized where needed for NDAs)
- OpenSprouts prominently featured
- Show the thinking and decisions, not just outcomes
- Screenshots, decisions made, lessons learned

### 4. Speaking & Collaboration
- Guest posts, podcast appearances, panels, co-written pieces
- Even if small right now, it establishes the pattern

### 5. Blog / Writing
- Mary's own voice on AI product thinking, PM craft
- Possibly landscape design and tech (ties to OpenSprouts)
- Long-term credibility builder and SEO asset

### 6. Uses / Stack
- Tools, frameworks, and resources Mary uses
- Signals depth to technical audiences without being a resume

### 7. Now Page
- What Mary is currently focused on (job search, OpenSprouts, etc.)
- Indie web convention that makes the site feel human

### 8. OpenSprouts Spotlight
- Dedicated section or sub-page, not just a portfolio card
- This is her most compelling current story
- Include waitlist integration if available

### 9. Case Studies (2-3 deep dives)
- Full breakdowns: problem, thinking process, decision, outcome
- Separates her from PMs who only have bullet point lists

### 10. Recommendations / Testimonials
- Pull 2-3 strong LinkedIn recommendations as quotes
- Social proof from real people

### 11. Resume / CV Download
- Simple PDF download link

### 12. Reading List / Resources
- Books, papers, resources that shaped her thinking
- Signals intellectual seriousness

### 13. Newsletter Signup
- Capture emails for future OpenSprouts launch or content
- Even before a newsletter exists, start collecting interest

### 14. Contact
- Email or a form, LinkedIn, GitHub
- Include a specific prompt: "I'm open to AI PM roles, speaking invitations, and early conversations about OpenSprouts."
- Tell people exactly when to reach out to reduce friction

### 15. Hobbies
- **Drawing/painting:** Show prominently, include actual artwork. Reinforces visual/aesthetic eye and ties to design interest in OpenSprouts.
- **Pistol shooting competitions:** Mention in text only ("I compete in pistol shooting competitions"). No photos. Guns are polarizing in tech; text reads as disciplined and interesting without triggering unconscious bias before someone knows her work.
